As per Regulation 39(4) read with Schedule VI  of the Listing Regulations, all shares issued pursuant to any issue which remain unclaimed shall be transferred by the Company to “Unclaimed Suspense Account”.   The Company has sent three reminders to the shareholders whose share certificates were returned undelivered. After processing of the valid claims received for release of undelivered Bonus Share Certificates, there were 2,547 shareholders whose 6,07,904 Bonus Shares in the aggregate were liable to be transferred to M&M Suspense Account. Pursuant to Schedule VI, these 6,07,904 unclaimed Bonus shares were transferred to M&M Suspense Account on 28th November, 2018.
