A person can act as a proxy on behalf of Members not exceeding fifty and holding in the aggregate not more than ten percent of the total share capital of the Company carrying voting rights. Proxies submitted on behalf of limited companies, societies, etc., must be supported by an appropriate resolution/authority as applicable.
